Frustrated with Patient Care ?

As as I former clinician. I know all too well how frustrating patient care can be. You're expected to take phone calls, treat multiple patients at once, manage cancellations and late arrivals, and write error free documentation, all while delivering strong outcomes and satisfaction. I always wished I had a tool to help me focus quickly amongst the chaos.

ClinNotes was designed with the above scenario in mind. Whether you're rattled from something earlier in the day, or just havent seen a scheduled patient for a few weeks. Clinnotes allows clincians to record and store patient and episode of care specific reflections and reminders. A glance at these prior to a patient visit can quickly refresh your memory about relevant patient details that you've specifically set. This way you no longer have to look back and read your previous notes and documentation as we all know it is optimized for billing and litigation, not skill acquisition and patient care.
